An inquisitive individual Jose Serra wants to apply his knowledge from the STEM field by helping individuals with his experience in Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning. Jose Serra has been to several research conferences, to name a few some include Great Minds in STEM (GMiS) in Pasadena, California, and the Garden State – Louis Stokes Association for Minority Participation (GS-LSAMP) in Rutgers University Newark and New Brunswick Campus, as well as at New Jersey Institute of Technology. As a young Puerto Rican, Jose Serra involves himself with namely with research projects, and internships. However, with the experience acquired at Panasonic Corporation of North America working with hardware, software, as well as seeing how both works together in real world applications. While developing applications for Panasonic Jose Serra found himself inspired by the company’s founder Konosuke Matsushita. Determined to make a difference for individuals that have neurodegenerative, neurocognitive, neurodevelopmental, and neurological conditions Jose is finding his way to achieving such a goal. One quote by the founder of Panasonic is “Improving people’s lives through creating goods needed for society or through providing wholehearted services are vitally important.” Taking that to heart Jose sees the importance of creating improved Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) technology using AI/ML/DL technology.
